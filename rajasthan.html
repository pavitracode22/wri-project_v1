PUBLICATION

Estimate Energy Storage Requirements for Rajasthan Grid

WRI India worked with Customised Energy Solutions and BASK Foundation on evaluating energy storage requirements for Rajasthan. The objective of the study was to assist the state identify energy storage requirements (till 2030) at the state level as well as applications for grid-connected battery energy storage systems and pumped hydro storage system with increased renewable integration.

GOVERNMENT PUBLICATION

Rajasthan Solar Energy Policy 2019

The Policy aims to achieve a target of 30,000 MW solar power projects upto 2024-25 in the state. This Policy also aims to promote solar energy through small decentralized grid connected solar power projects at load centres, rooftop solar projects, solar water pumps, solar projects with storage systems, and solar parks.
