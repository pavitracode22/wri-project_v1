Program For Responsible Energy Management

In partnership with Tamil Nadu Urban Finance & Infrastructure Development Corporation Ltd. (TUFIDCO), Auroville Consulting organized a series of five three-day training sessions on Responsible Energy Management for municipal engineers from Tamil Nadu.

n total a 192 Municipal Engineers from Tamil Nadu participated in this program. Energy conservation and efficiency as well as distributed energy solutions for municipal projects with focus on pumping systems, building energy efficiency and rooftop solar PV were addressed in the context of achieving responsible demand-supply management that will enable sustainable and equitable economic growth in India. This was a highly interactive program format that included lectures, site visits, financial calculations for energy efficiency interventions and solar PV installations.

07-FEB-2019

Auroville Consulting Kalpana, Crown Road, Auroville, Tamil Nadu
