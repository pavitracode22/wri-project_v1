Sustainable Energy Transformation (SET) is an initiative to (a) support in development and/or implementation of Renewable Energy policies, (b) conduct research and analysis to support state’s clean energy future, and (c) develop stakeholder engagement strategies for a sustainable energy transformation in the states of Tamil Nadu, Rajasthan and Kerala.
